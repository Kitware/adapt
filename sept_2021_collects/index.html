September 2021 data collects in Alaska

Kitware and ACUASI tested our payload at the Tanana River and the Chena River. These flights were focused on making sure the payload hardware and software are fully functional and support real-time deep-neural-network procession. After we analyze the data that we have collected, we will make it available for public access.
