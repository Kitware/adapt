Payload Bill of Materials

  • Embedded Computer with GPU ($750 *): Nvidia Jetson Xavier AGX
  • Color Camera option 1 (global shutter):
    • Global shutter ($1750): FLIR Blackfly S GigE BFS-PGE-161S7C-C
    • Lens ($1078): Edmund Optics #86-569
  • Color Camera option 2 (rolling shutter **):
    • Rolling shutter ($780): FLIR Blackfly S GigE BFS-PGE-200S6C-C
    • Lens ($550): Computar V0826-MPZ
  • Inertial Navigation System ($3230): Advanced Navigation Spatial
  • Wireless communications:
    • On payload ($182): airMAX Bullet AC Dual-Band IP67 Radio
    • Antenna on payload ($15): N-Type Dual Band
    • Bridge at Base station ($80): Ubiquiti LBE-5AC-GEN2-US LiteBeam Wireless
  • Other Required components:
    • M.2 NVMe SSD 1TB ($150): Samsung 970 EVO
    • PoE injector for wireless ($8): Passive single port injector
    • PCIe Network card ($27): STARTECH 1-PORT PCI EXPRSS GIGABIT
    • Cable for camera power ($67): 6-pin GPIO Hirose Connector
    • Carbon fiber frame ($500): (Custom - Open Hardware CAD files to be uploaded)
  • Misc:
    • Antenna adapter ($5): N Female to N Female
    • Level converter ($18): Bi-Directional Level converter
    • (Mounting hardware)
    • (RJ45 Network cables)
    • (Misc. wiring and connectors)
    • (Misc. frame mounts, stand-offs, misc screws)

Total components Cost: $6574 - $8060

Open Hardware

We will be updating this soon. In the spirit of Open Hardware, all the hardware components were selected from off-the-shelf components.

* Approximate cost at time of purchase - some prices may vary (prices in USD including taxes and shipping)

** The rolling shutter version of the camera is a cheaper option. However, it is not recommended if you intend to exploit the imagery for structure from motion (e.g., TeleSculptor).
